The idea for Bux Superstore germinated during one of our fruitless online shopping trips. We realised that though online marketplaces have grown by leaps and bounds, within the last few years, the picture wasn’t yet complete. Limited shipping locations, low-quality products, exorbitant exchange rates – these were just a few of the worries plaguing online shoppers.

We decided that it was time to sort out these worries. We rolled up our sleeves and got down to brainstorming – voila, the Bux Superstore was born.
